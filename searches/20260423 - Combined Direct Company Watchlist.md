## Practical Use

If time is constrained, search in this order:

1. `Tier A` companies with current remote or flexible openings in your lane
2. `Tier A` companies with strong brand + role coherence but mild work-model risk
3. `Tier B` moonshots or prestige names only when a role is unusually strong

When the search is thin, this list should still be checked directly even if aggregators are quiet.
