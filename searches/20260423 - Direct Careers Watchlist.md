# 2026-04-23 Direct Careers Watchlist

Purpose: curated brand-name software / AI / cloud companies worth checking directly on their official careers pages, biased toward:

- recognizable resume signal
- plausible fit for `Director / Senior Director / Head / Principal / Lead Architect` lanes
- compensation that can plausibly work
- remote or at least not obviously impossible work models

## Tier A: Check 2-3x/Week

These are the strongest direct-check companies because they combine cachet, plausible fit, and a work model that is at least potentially workable.

### Atlassian

- Official careers: https://www.atlassian.com/company/careers
- Why watch: major collaboration-software brand; strong platform, product, AI, and engineering-leadership halo
- Work model: `Team Anywhere`; distributed-friendly
- Best role families: `Director of Engineering`, `Senior Engineering Manager`, `Principal Engineer`, `Platform`, `Developer Experience`, `AI`

### GitHub

- Official careers: https://www.github.careers/
- Why watch: elite developer-platform brand; strong fit for platform, productivity, AI-assisted engineering, and collaboration-tooling roles
- Work model: remote-friendly signal on careers page
- Best role families: `Platform`, `Developer Productivity`, `Engineering Leadership`, `AI`, `Developer Experience`

### GitLab

- Official careers: https://about.gitlab.com/jobs/
- Why watch: still one of the cleanest brand-name all-remote software companies; strong DevSecOps / platform / engineering-systems fit
- Work model: all-remote
- Best role families: `Director`, `Senior Manager`, `Platform`, `Security`, `Developer Productivity`, `Principal`

### Cloudflare

- Official careers: https://www.cloudflare.com/careers/
- Jobs: https://www.cloudflare.com/careers/jobs
- Why watch: major infrastructure / security / internet platform brand with real technical cachet
- Work model: hybrid, distributed, and in-office options
- Best role families: `Platform`, `Reliability`, `Security`, `Traffic / Networking`, `Developer Platform`, `Engineering Leadership`

### Grafana Labs

- Official careers: https://grafana.com/about/careers/
- Open positions: https://grafana.com/about/careers/open-positions/
- Why watch: strong observability / open-source / platform cachet and unusually good remote posture
- Work model: majority of roles fully remote; remote-first
- Best role families: `Director`, `Engineering Manager`, `Platform`, `Observability`, `Databases`, `Developer Systems`

### 1Password

- Official careers: https://1password.com/careers
- Jobs: https://1password.com/jobs
- Why watch: beloved security brand, strong identity / access / agent-security angle, good reputation, good role coherence with current search
- Work model: remote-first with intentional in-person collaboration
- Best role families: `Director`, `Senior Director`, `Platform`, `Identity`, `Security Infrastructure`, `Engineering Management`

### Stripe

- Official careers: https://stripe.com/jobs
- Search: https://stripe.com/jobs/search
- Why watch: top-tier engineering brand with real future-resume signal; strong platform and developer-infrastructure halo
- Work model: mixed; some U.S. remote roles exist, many roles are hub-based
- Best role families: `Platform`, `Developer Experience`, `Infrastructure`, `Payments`, `Data Platform`, `Engineering Leadership`

### Datadog

- Official careers: https://careers.datadoghq.com/
- Why watch: major observability / security platform brand with very strong technical cachet
- Work model: mixed; remote exists, but many roles are hybrid
- Best role families: `Observability`, `Platform`, `Security`, `Infra`, `Engineering Leadership`, `Principal`

### MongoDB

- Official careers: https://www.mongodb.com/careers
- Why watch: strong developer-data-platform brand; durable signal for platform / product / infra / engineering leadership
- Work model: multiple working models, including work-from-home flexibility depending on role
- Best role families: `Platform`, `Data`, `Developer Productivity`, `Engineering Leadership`, `Principal`

### Confluent

- Official careers: https://careers.confluent.io/
- U.S. jobs: https://careers.confluent.io/jobs/united_states
- Why watch: excellent distributed-systems / data-in-motion brand; high credibility for principal/platform lanes
- Work model: many remote U.S. roles
- Best role families: `Director`, `Platform`, `Streaming`, `Data Infrastructure`, `Security`, `Principal`

### Elastic

- Official careers: https://www.elastic.co/careers/
- Why watch: strong search / observability / security / open-source brand
- Work model: remote, hybrid, and in-person roles; distributed by design
- Best role families: `Search`, `Platform`, `Observability`, `Security`, `Engineering Leadership`, `Principal`

### Vercel

- Official careers: https://vercel.com/careers
- Why watch: cool modern developer-platform brand with strong AI and frontend-platform halo
- Work model: mixed; some `United States` roles are remote, many are hub-based
- Best role families: `Platform`, `AI`, `Developer Experience`, `Infrastructure`, `Engineering Management`

### Figma

- Official careers: https://www.figma.com/careers/
- Why watch: very strong product + collaboration + developer-tools cachet; especially relevant for collaborative-surface roles
- Work model: hiring across global hubs and remotely in the U.S. and Canada
- Best role families: `Engineering Manager`, `Observability`, `Search`, `Developer Tools`, `AI Product`, `Collaboration`

### YNAB

- Official jobs: https://ynab.pinpointhq.com/
- Why watch: smaller but very recognizable and well-liked brand; unusually strong remote culture and product affection
- Work model: explicitly `100% remote` on careers site
- Best role families: `Engineering Leadership`, `Platform`, `Security`, `Product Engineering`

## Tier B: Check Weekly

These have major cachet, but either the fit is narrower or the work-model risk is higher.

### OpenAI

- Official careers: https://openai.com/careers/
- Open roles: https://openai.com/careers/search/
- Why watch: maximum AI cachet; strong future-signal if a plausible role appears
- Work model: mixed; many listings are location-specific
- Best role families: `Applied AI Engineering`, `Platform`, `Infrastructure`, `Product`, `Security`

### Anthropic

- Official careers: https://www.anthropic.com/careers
- Why watch: top-tier AI cachet and strong alignment with AI-assisted tooling / systems work
- Work model: Bay Area-heavy; site says many staff are local and some others come in one week per month
- Best role families: `Engineering`, `Platform`, `Security`, `Applied AI`, `Systems`

### NVIDIA

- Official careers: https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/about-nvidia/careers/
- Why watch: very high AI / infrastructure / systems cachet
- Work model: more office/hardware/hub-centric; less likely to be remote-friendly
- Best role families: `AI Infrastructure`, `Developer Platforms`, `Systems`, `Cloud`, `Enterprise AI`

### Netflix

- Official careers: https://jobs.netflix.com/
- Engineering: https://jobs.netflix.com/careers/engineering
- Why watch: elite brand and strong platform / reliability / experimentation / product-infra halo
- Work model: engineering page emphasizes major engineering hubs in the U.S. and Poland
- Best role families: `Infrastructure`, `Product Platform`, `Security`, `Engineering Management`, `Principal`

### Snowflake

- Official careers: https://careers.snowflake.com/
- AI engineering: https://careers.snowflake.com/us/en/ai-ml-engineering
- Why watch: premium data / AI platform brand with strong technical credibility
- Work model: more location-centric than the remote-first companies above
- Best role families: `Data Platform`, `AI Platform`, `Developer Platform`, `Infrastructure`, `Principal`

### Miro

- Official careers: https://us.miro.com/careers/
- Open positions: https://us.miro.com/careers/open-positions/
- Why watch: respected collaboration / innovation-workspace brand; direct thematic relevance to collaboration-platform roles
- Work model: mixed; some remote roles, many location-specific
- Best role families: `Collaboration`, `Platform`, `AI`, `Product Engineering`, `Technical Program / Leadership`
