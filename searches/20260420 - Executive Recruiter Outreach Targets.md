## Wave 1: Highest-Priority Outreach

### 1. Riviera Partners

- Best contact / entry path: `Iain Grant`, Senior Partner, Public Companies; also use Riviera's candidate contact path.
- Why this is high priority: Riviera is tightly focused on technical executive search across `software engineering`, `AI/ML/Data`, `IT`, and related leadership functions. This is one of the cleanest fits for remote software-first `CTO` / `VP Engineering` / `Head of Engineering` searches.

- Notes: Best fit when the target is SaaS, platform, AI, PE-backed software, or late-stage/public-company tech leadership.

## Positioning To Use Consistently

Use some version of this in every outreach:

> I’m a hands-on software and engineering executive with 20+ years leading product/application engineering, modernization, cloud-native platform work, and practical AI delivery. I’m focused on CTO, Head of Engineering, VP Engineering, and strong Senior Director opportunities, primarily in software-first and workflow-heavy businesses. Recent outcomes include scaling engineering from 6 to roughly 35, cutting a modernization cycle from 4 months to 3 weeks with AI-assisted workflows, reducing non-production cloud spend 35-40%, and leading ISO 27001/security work.

Tailor the last sentence by firm:

- `Riviera / True / SPMB`: emphasize software-first, AI, product/application engineering.
- `JM Search / RRA / Korn Ferry`: emphasize executive leadership, transformation, modernization, governance, and PE/public-company readiness.
- `Angus / Gilman / Emerald`: emphasize Cincinnati base, regional flexibility, and willingness to consider strong hybrid or remote-with-travel opportunities.
