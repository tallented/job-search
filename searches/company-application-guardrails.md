# Company Application Guardrails

Use this file during search triage before promoting another opening at a company where Chris already has recent application history.

Default rule:

- If a company has a recent submission, prefer one strongest role or a tightly coherent pair.
- Deprioritize additional openings unless the company invites Chris to apply, a recruiter redirects him, or the new role is materially stronger and clearly coherent with the prior submission.
- When in doubt, record the role in a search queue as `Hold / Company coherence` rather than creating a new resume folder.
