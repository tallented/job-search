# Search Sites

Last updated: 2026-04-09

This file lists the main search channels to use beyond Indeed and LinkedIn, with an emphasis on:

- senior engineering leadership
- principal / staff-plus technical roles
- remote or remote-friendly work
- fractional / interim / consulting opportunities
- AI, cloud, platform, security, and modernization work

Use this file as the channel inventory. The recurring search cadence and triage workflow live in `AGENTS.md`.

## Core Full-Time Channels

### Built In

- URL: `https://builtin.com/jobs`
- Remote jobs: `https://builtin.com/jobs/remote`
- Why use it:
  - tech-focused rather than general-purpose
  - strong company profiles, compensation visibility, and role summaries
  - useful for principal, staff-plus, engineering manager, director, platform, security, and AI roles
- Best for:
  - remote bridge roles at recognizable tech companies
  - principal / expert / leader roles
  - platform, AI, DevSecOps, infra, and product-engineering roles

### Wellfound

- URL: `https://wellfound.com/`
- Why use it:
  - startup-focused
  - salary and equity are often visible
  - direct access to founders / small hiring teams
- Best for:
  - startup and scale-up roles
  - AI-native companies
  - high-upside stretch opportunities

### Y Combinator Work at a Startup

- URL: `https://www.workatastartup.com/jobs`
- Why use it:
  - direct line into YC startup hiring
  - many AI, infrastructure, and platform companies
  - useful for remote startup searches and founder-led companies
- Best for:
  - AI startups
  - strong builder / player-coach roles
  - smaller companies where breadth and speed matter

### Himalayas

- URL: `https://himalayas.app/`
- Docs: `https://himalayas.app/docs/how-remote-jobs-work-on-himalayas`
- Why use it:
  - remote-first board
  - good timezone/location filtering
  - jobs sourced from direct posts and ATS/career pages, then reviewed
- Best for:
  - remote-only filtering
  - company discovery
  - supplementing LinkedIn / Indeed with cleaner remote search

### FlexJobs

- URL: `https://www.flexjobs.com/`
- Remote jobs: `https://www.flexjobs.com/remote-jobs`
- Why use it:
  - curated remote / hybrid / flexible work board
  - better signal-to-noise than generic boards
- Caution:
  - paid access
- Best for:
  - remote-only searches
  - fallback / coverage search when broadening the funnel

## Company-Site / ATS First

Use direct company sites when targeting known companies. Prioritize:

- Workday company boards
- Greenhouse boards
- Lever boards
- Ashby boards
- direct careers pages

Why:

- freshest job state
- fewer aggregator errors
- easier to validate whether the req is still open

## Fractional / Interim / Consulting Channels

### Fractional Jobs

- URL: `https://www.fractionaljobs.io/`
- Why use it:
  - purpose-built for fractional retainers
  - engineering and CTO are common categories
  - good learning content on fractional positioning and pricing
- Best for:
  - fractional CTO
  - interim engineering leadership
  - part-time AI / architecture / platform leadership

### fraction.al

- URL: `https://fraction.al/`
- Why use it:
  - specifically focused on matching startups with fractional CTOs
  - direct thematic fit for current positioning
- Best for:
  - fractional CTO
  - hands-on architecture leadership
  - startup bridge work

### Braintrust

- Join: `https://www.usebraintrust.com/join`
- Overview: `https://support.usebraintrust.com/hc/en-us/articles/14303151864343-What-is-Braintrust`
- Why use it:
  - high-paying independent technical work
  - talent keeps 100% of earnings
  - good for engineering / AI / platform / architect work
- Best for:
  - contract and consulting work
  - higher-end freelance engineering and AI projects

### Catalant

- Independent consultants: `https://catalant.com/for-independent-consultants/`
- Why use it:
  - higher-end independent consulting marketplace
  - good enterprise access
  - strong fit for strategy + architecture + digital/AI transformation work
- Best for:
  - AI transformation
  - architecture / modernization consulting
  - fractional / interim executive problem-solving

### Business Talent Group (BTG)

- About: `https://businesstalentgroup.com/about-btg/`
- Enterprise / marketplace overview: `https://businesstalentgroup.com/enterprise/`
- Why use it:
  - high-end on-demand talent marketplace
  - interim executives and senior independent consultants
  - stronger enterprise / transformation angle than gig platforms
- Best for:
  - interim executive roles
  - strategy + technology transformation work
  - high-end consulting rather than staff augmentation

### A.Team

- URL: `https://www.a.team/`
- Expert talent: `https://www.a.team/expert-talent`
- Why use it:
  - curated network
  - strong AI and platform emphasis
  - good for embedded expert work and rapid-deployment teams
- Best for:
  - AI systems and product/platform build work
  - specialist consulting / embedded leadership

## Practical Notes

- Use browser automation at a human pace for research and discovery, not mass scraping.
- Do not auto-apply.
- Use these channels to build a review queue, then tailor only the strongest opportunities.
- For brand-name companies, always prefer the official careers site over an aggregator once a role looks promising.
