## Quick “Who To Contact” Checklist

- Look for: Partner/Principal/Managing Director in Technology, Software, Digital, Product Engineering, or “CTO/CIO practice.”
- Prefer: individuals who have placed CTO/VP Eng roles at Series A+ and established SaaS companies.
- Ask directly: what active searches they have that match remote-first software leadership, and whether your comp floor is realistic for their client mix.
