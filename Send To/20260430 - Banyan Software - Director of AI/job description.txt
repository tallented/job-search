Banyan Software - Director of AI
AI Factory - Application Modernization Program

Department: AI Factory - Banyan Software
Reports To: VP of Engineering
Location: Remote (US-based preferred, 4+ hrs overlap US Eastern)
Type: Full-Time
Direct Reports: Forward Deployed Engineers, Project Manager / Scrum Master
Travel: Up to 15% (operating company site visits as needed)
Compensation: $250,000-$300,000 base, excluding annual bonus and equity when applicable

Role notes:
- Lead Banyan's centralized AI Factory program for application modernization across 100+ operating companies.
- Modernize 15-25 year-old codebases into cloud-native, mobile-first, always-on SaaS platforms.
- Use AI-powered discovery, verification-first engineering, and an integrated delivery model to rewrite legacy applications in 15-25 weeks versus traditional 2-5 year efforts.
- Own program strategy, delivery cadence, forward-deployed engineering, cloud operations, platform automation, stakeholder alignment, and executive communication.
- Build and scale the FDE team from scratch, author the engagement playbook, and personally run early transformations as a player-coach.
- Oversee multi-cloud architecture, CI/CD automation, observability, security, resiliency, and DevSecOps practices.
- Define and track KPIs, velocity metrics, and business-value outcomes for OpCo CEOs, CTOs, product leaders, Banyan's VP Engineering, and executive leadership.
- Drive the MACROSS framework: Maintainability, Adaptability, Cost Accountability, Reliability, Observability, Scalability, Security.

Required:
- 10+ years progressive software engineering experience, with at least 3 years spanning technical delivery and program management.
- Application modernization, re-platforming, or digital transformation experience, ideally across multiple products or companies.
- Ability to embed with or lead cross-functional engineering teams without formal authority.
- Hands-on cloud-native architecture experience with AWS, Azure, or GCP, CI/CD, IaC, and DevSecOps.
- Experience building teams, processes, and playbooks from scratch in startup-like environments.
- Strong executive communication and C-level stakeholder presentation experience.

Preferred:
- Forward-deployed engineering, professional services, or embedded consulting.
- Private equity portfolio operations, multi-entity software, or vertical-market software.
- AI-assisted development tools such as Cursor, Claude Code, GitHub Copilot, or agent-based development.
- Legacy application assessment, strangler-fig patterns, or monolith-to-microservices transformations.
- Agile coaching or program management background.
