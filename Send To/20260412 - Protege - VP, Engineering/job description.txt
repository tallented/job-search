Company: Protege
Title: VP, Engineering
Employment Type: Full time
Location: Remote

Company Overview

Protege is building to solve what it describes as the biggest unmet need in AI: getting access to the right training data. The process today is time intensive, expensive, and often fails. The Protege platform facilitates the secure, efficient, and privacy-centric exchange of AI training data.

The company positions AI training data exchange as a generational opportunity, says it is backed by major investors, and describes itself as a lean, fast-moving, high-trust team built for people who thrive on ambiguity, own outcomes, and want to shape the future of data and AI.

Key Responsibilities

Engineering Leadership & Strategy

- Define and execute a long-term technical strategy aligned with company goals.
- Partner with executive leadership on product direction, innovation, and roadmap execution.
- Establish engineering-wide structures, processes, and operating rhythms that support growth at scale.
- Partner cross-functionally to anticipate what the company and engineering organization will need as it scales, and proactively seed the right processes, capacity, and operating rhythms.
- Set the standard for technical excellence and execution, and build a high-trust culture where teams take ownership and move quickly with rigor.

Team Building & People Leadership

- Recruit, develop, and retain top engineering talent; act as a talent magnet in the market.
- Partner with People on engineering recruiting strategy and take ownership of process and outcomes.
- Mentor engineering managers and technical leaders to support career growth and strong technical judgment.
- Create a collaborative environment that fosters diversity of thought, high ownership, accountability, and continuous improvement.

Execution, Quality & Delivery

- Own engineering execution end-to-end, from planning through delivery quality and operating cadence.
- Build and refine engineering workflows including code review, testing automation, observability, and incident management.
- Safeguard product quality by building processes and infrastructure that ensure reliability, security, and performance, while fostering customer-experience ownership.

Product & Technology Ownership

- Oversee architectural decisions, infrastructure scaling, and system design.
- Work closely with product leadership to translate customer needs into scalable product experiences.
- Champion a high bar for innovation and help the company build functionality ahead of customer demand in the AI data infrastructure space.

Must-Have Experience

- 10+ years in engineering roles, with 4+ years in leadership managing multiple teams or organizations.
- Fluency in setting technical strategy in high-growth or early-stage settings and aligning technical direction to business needs.
- Track record of growing and leading multi-level engineering organizations of 30+ with strong operational rigor and technical standards.
- Demonstrated ability to build impressive, user-centric products in fast-paced environments.
- Strong background in engineering discipline, quality practices, and operational structure.
- Exceptional communication, cross-functional leadership, and decision-making skills.
