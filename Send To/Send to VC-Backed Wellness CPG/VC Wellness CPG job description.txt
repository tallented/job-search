Chief Technology Officer (CTO) | VC-Backed Wellness CPG | Fully Remote 

Introduction

Our client is a rapidly growing consumer health and wellness brand built around the belief that the body has an extraordinary ability to restore, repair, and thrive when given the right foundation. With a science-backed approach and a mission to empower people to live healthier, more resilient lives, the company is redefining modern wellness through innovation and integrity.

The team is made up of builders who thrive on progress, collaboration, and purpose. They move fast, operate with ownership, and are united by a shared vision to make a lasting impact in the health and wellness space.


The Role

The Chief Technology Officer (CTO) will lead the strategy, development, and scaling of the technology systems that power the company’s next phase of growth. As a member of the executive team, this leader will oversee all aspects of eCommerce, data architecture, infrastructure, and emerging technology innovation.


The CTO will collaborate cross-functionally with Marketing, Operations, and Finance to deliver seamless digital experiences, enhance data-driven decision-making, and enable scalable omnichannel growth. This role is ideal for a hands-on technical leader who thrives in fast-moving, consumer-facing environments and is motivated by building high-performing teams and impactful systems.


Responsibilities

    Develop and execute a multi-year technology roadmap to support company growth, scalability, and global expansion.
    Oversee eCommerce architecture (Shopify Plus) to ensure site performance, checkout optimization, and reliable integrations.
    Build, mentor, and scale high-performing engineering, data, and DevOps teams focused on innovation, reliability, and security.
    Own the company’s data infrastructure using tools like GTM, BigQuery, and related platforms, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and accessibility.
    Create a unified data foundation to enhance analytics, LTV modeling, and personalization initiatives.
    Introduce automation and AI tools to improve marketing, customer experience, and supply chain operations.
    Ensure ongoing compliance with global data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, CPRA) and platform security standards.
    Evaluate and adopt emerging technologies (AI, composable commerce, personalization engines) to elevate the digital experience.
