Questions
About the Role
- [ ] Can you tell me more about the specific role?
- [ ] Title of the role
- [ ] What are the current and future responsibilities of this role?
- [ ] Who does this report to?
    - [ ] Can you describe the working style and background of the person I would report to?
- [ ] How many direct and indirect reports are there to this role?
    - [ ] What is the current structure and morale of the team I would be leading?
    - [ ] What are their key strengths and challenges?
- [ ] Who are the primary business stakeholders this role will partner with?
- [ ] Is this a new position or being filled?
    - [ ] If this role is being filled, what led to the departure of the previous incumbent?

Technology
- [ ] Is the current technology “stable” or is there ongoing modernization or new builds?
    - [ ] State of technical debt?
    - [ ] How does the organization balance the need for speed/agility (to meet client demands) with the requirement for safety/stability in operational technology?
    - [ ] What is the organization's appetite for investing in paying down technical debt vs. building new features?
- [ ] What would I be overseeing?
    - [ ] Owner-facing technology?
    - [ ] Logistics technology?
- [ ] Cloud based?  Provider?  Hybrid?
- [ ] Software tech stacks?
- [ ] What is the IT budget size relative to the overall company revenue, and how are strategic investment decisions made?

What does Success Look like?
- [ ] How does the company define success for this role over the next 12–18 months?”
- [ ] Are there specific metrics and deliverables will this role be measured by?
    - [ ] Are there 1-3 specific KPI for the first year?
- [ ] What is the biggest roadblock or obstacle the previous person/team faced, and how is the company prepared to help this new leader overcome it?

Career Progression
- [ ] Over time what could my career look like?
    - [ ] What is the next logic step for this position over time?

Benefits
- [ ] What is the salary range? What does the full compensation structure look like?
    - [ ] Bonuses
    - [ ] 401k, match and time to vest
- [ ] PTO, Sick, Holiday?
    - [ ] How does PTO work in practice for senior leaders?
- [ ] Is this considered an Executive role within the organization?  Are there Executive specific benefits?
- [ ] Are there equity grants (RSUs or stock options, even if NetJets is private, Berkshire Hathaway may have programs) and long-term incentive plans (LTIPs)
- [ ] Are there any non-standard executive travel benefits or fractional jet program perks associated with this level of leadership?
- [ ] Other benefits

Company Culture
- [ ] How would you describe the company culture?
    - [ ] How well does the current culture support change leadership?
- [ ] Is this Hybrid or completely In-Office?
- [ ] What is the typical dress code and office environment like?

Typical Day
- [ ] What does a typical day look like for this role and in relation to other key stakeholders?
    - [ ] Meeting schedule?
