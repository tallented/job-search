Full job description

The Chief Technical Officer (CTO) at AbsenceSoft serves as the strategic leader for all technology initiatives, driving innovation, architecture, security, and operational excellence in alignment with business objectives. This role oversees the design, development, and deployment of cutting-edge leave management software solutions, ensuring scalability, security, and user experience meet the demands of a global market. As a member of the executive leadership team, the CTO shapes long-term technology vision, leads engineering and IT operations, and partners with cross-functional leaders to deliver transformative capabilities to customers worldwide.
