## Resume Dispatch Legend

- `Customize`: spend one of the daily custom-resume slots because the role is strong enough, differentiated enough, or senior enough to justify a req-specific draft.
- `Light customize`: start from the named canonical resume and update the headline, summary, core skills, and 2-4 top bullets; do not over-invest before comp/work-model/source risk is cleared.
- `Canonical direct`: submit the named canonical resume as-is or with only application-form/cover-note adjustments; use this for speed or lower-priority fallback roles.
- `Hold/skip`: do not spend resume time unless a constraint changes or the live source/comp is validated.
