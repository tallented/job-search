About the Role

Grafana Cloud is Grafana Labs' composable observability platform for metrics, logs, traces, and profiles. The Databases department owns and operates the telemetry databases that power Grafana Cloud, including Mimir, Loki, Tempo, and Pyroscope. The Logs group is responsible for Grafana Cloud Logs, Loki OSS, and Grafana Enterprise Logs.

Loki is described as a cloud-native database built in Go, delivered as microservices, and backed by object storage for logs observability at scale.

What You'll Be Doing
- Lead a team of 20+ engineers and grow it over time, including managing managers, developing engineers, providing regular feedback, and supporting growth through career conversations.
- Collaborate with product, design, and engineering leadership to define goals that move the Logs group forward and align with broader business objectives.
- Drive product vision by listening to customer feedback and anticipating market trends.
- Provide guidance across the full project lifecycle from ideation through planning, execution, and post-launch follow-through.
- Foster a psychologically safe environment where engineers can learn, experiment, and iterate quickly.
- Work across team boundaries and partner with other functions to solve customer problems.

What Makes You a Great Fit
- Strong director-level engineering people manager with an entrepreneurial vision.
- Bias toward action and ownership in ambiguous, complex problem spaces.
- Comfortable collaborating across time zones and cultures in a fully remote environment.
- Demonstrated ability to manage complexity, align stakeholders, and elevate team performance.
- Solid understanding of distributed-systems principles including scalability, fault tolerance, consistency, and observability.
- Comfort with AI-assisted tooling and practical experience folding AI-powered tools into a team's workflow.

Why Grafana / Notes
