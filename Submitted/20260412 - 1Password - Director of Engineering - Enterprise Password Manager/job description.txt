About 1Password

At 1Password, we’re building the foundation for a safe, productive digital future. Our mission is to unleash employee productivity without compromising security by ensuring every identity is authentic, every application sign-in is secure, and every device is trusted. We innovated the market-leading enterprise password manager and pioneered Extended Access Management, a new cybersecurity category built for the way people and AI agents work today. As one of the most loved brands in cybersecurity, we take a human-centric approach in everything from product strategy to user experience. Over 180,000 businesses, from Fortune 100 leaders to the world’s most innovative AI companies, trust 1Password to help their teams securely adopt the SaaS and AI tools they need to do their best work.

If you're excited about the opportunity to contribute to the digital safety of millions, to work alongside a team of curious, driven individuals, and to solve hard problems in a fast-paced, dynamic environment, then we want to hear from you. Come join us and help shape a safer, simpler digital future.

At 1Password, we’re looking for a Director of Engineering to lead the strategy, delivery, and evolution of 1Password's core enterprise credentials platform. This role owns how enterprises securely manage human access at scale—balancing best-in-class usability with uncompromising security, privacy, and trust.

You will lead engineers and partner with a cross-functional organization of product managers, designers, and go-to-market specialists responsible for building and operating the systems that give customers visibility into credentials, secrets, and sign-ins across their workforce, devices, and environments. Your team's work enables enterprises to reduce security risk, prevent breaches, and improve productivity while integrating seamlessly with existing IAM, security, and developer workflows.

This leader will translate 1Password's business and product strategy into an actionable roadmap, delivering capabilities that enable enterprises to securely manage access at scale. The ideal candidate combines deep experience in enterprise security and identity management, strong organizational and people leadership, and the ability to work effectively across Product, Engineering, Security, and Customer Success.

This is a remote opportunity within Canada and the US.

What we're looking for:

-

Lead and scale the Enterprise Password Management organization: Set and align vision and strategy with company priorities; build and organize teams; partner cross-functionally to establish roadmaps; and own delivery execution for enterprise credential management capabilities.

-

Deliver secure, scalable, and intuitive credential management: Drive the direction and outcomes for enterprise password management, ensuring deep integration with IAM, SSO, SIEM, and developer tooling ecosystems. Enable high-quality decision-making by balancing data, speed, judgment, and risk, while empowering teams to own outcomes.

-

Uphold and evolve 1Password's zero-knowledge security model: Ensure the platform maintains uncompromising security standards while delivering exceptional usability. Partner with Security and Compliance teams to meet enterprise requirements and regulatory standards.

-

Product leadership and excellence: Own the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for Enterprise Password Management. Ensure the platform scales with customer needs and aligns with 1Password's compliance commitments and enterprise go-to-market strategy.

-

Cross-functional partnership: Collaborate closely with Engineering, Security, Platform, Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success to support enterprise adoption, customer outcomes, and revenue growth.

-

Grow and develop talent: Recruit, mentor, and retain top product and engineering talent; foster a culture of curiosity, innovation, ownership, and business impact; and develop leadership depth by coaching senior leaders and proactively shaping future workforce capabilities.

What you can expect:

-

Support and set the strategic direction for teams delivering software that contributes to our mission. You'll collaborate with senior leaders to ensure your team’s objectives align with the company’s long-term goals.

-

Grow technical contributors and managers to reach their full potential. Recruit and retain top talent in a fast-growing environment, partnering with the Talent team to build high-performing engineering teams.

-

Whether you're driving product growth, improving user adoption, scaling integrations, or enhancing developer tools, you'll own the delivery of high-impact projects that matter to our customers.

-

Work closely with product, design, and other teams to create an engaging technical vision for your area. You'll resolve conflicts, drive alignment, and ensure your team’s work supports broader company objectives.

-

Operate autonomously, prioritize effectively, and make judgment calls on complex, multi-team initiatives. Your ability to scope and lead projects will be key to your success.

Our culture
At 1Password, we prioritize collaboration, clear and transparent communication, receptiveness to feedback, and alignment with our core values: keep it simple, lead with honesty, and put people first.

You’ll be part of a team that challenges the status quo, and is excited to experiment and iterate in search of the best solution. That said, 1Password is not for everyone. Our work is demanding, we strive for excellence, and the pace is fast. We need people who are keen to take on challenging problems, who seek feedback to grow, and who are driven to make an impact.
