The Role

The Senior Vice President of IT Strategy & Innovation leads the company's technology direction, focusing on strategy, innovation, and enterprise architecture while championing AI, data, and interoperability.

Built on meritocracy, our unique company culture rewards self-starters and those who are committed to doing what is best for our customers.

Brown & Brown is seeking Senior Vice President, IT Strategy & Innovation to join our growing team in Daytona, FL, Plano, TX, Boston, MA, or Remote.

The Senior Vice President of IT Strategy & Innovation is a visionary technology executive responsible for shaping the long-term technology direction of the enterprise while driving near-term innovation and incubation. This leader will define and execute an integrated IT strategy aligned with corporate objectives, advance enterprise architecture, and champion innovation with a strong emphasis on data, AI, and interoperability.

As a direct report to the CITO, this role influences the entire technology landscape and plays a critical part in enabling business growth through modern, scalable, and forward-thinking solutions.

How You Will Contribute

- Develop and maintain a comprehensive, multi-year IT strategy that is tightly aligned with the company's business vision, priorities, and growth objectives.
- Ensure technology strategy directly enables business outcomes, operational excellence, and competitive differentiation.
- Lead the enterprise innovation agenda, with a strong focus on AI, automation, data-driven capabilities, and next-generation digital experiences.
- Balance long-term strategic planning with agile, iterative incubation efforts that accelerate value creation.
- Oversee the Enterprise Architecture function, ensuring cohesive architectural standards, frameworks, and policies across the organization.
- Establish and maintain architectural guidelines that promote interoperability, scalability, security, and modernization.
- Partner with technology and business leaders to ensure architectural decisions support business outcomes and future-state capabilities.
- Identify, evaluate, and incubate emerging technologies, especially in AI, data platforms, and integration ecosystems, to drive competitive advantage.
- Build and lead innovation programs, labs, and pilots that rapidly test, validate, and prove value for new concepts.
- Foster a culture of experimentation, continuous learning, and responsible innovation.
- Lead, mentor, and inspire a diverse team of technologists, architects, and innovators across the enterprise.
- Build organizational capability in AI, data engineering, architecture, and modern technology practices.
- Champion career development, technical excellence, and a culture of collaboration and empowerment.
- Serve as a brilliant communicator who can translate complex technology concepts into clear, compelling narratives for all audiences, from engineers to the Board.
- Partner closely with business leaders to co-create technology-enabled strategies that drive growth, efficiency, and transformation.
- Represent IT Strategy & Innovation in executive forums, steering committees, and cross-functional initiatives.

Skills & Experience to be Successful

- 15+ years of progressive leadership experience in technology strategy, enterprise architecture, innovation, or related domains.
- Deep expertise in data platforms, AI/ML, interoperability, and modern integration patterns.
- Proven track record of developing and executing enterprise-level technology strategies.
- Demonstrated success leading innovation programs and incubating emerging technologies.
- Exceptional people leader with a passion for developing high-performing teams and nurturing technical talent.
- Outstanding communicator capable of engaging technical and non-technical audiences with clarity, confidence, and influence.
- Strong collaborator who builds trusted relationships across business and technology functions.
- Strategic thinker with the ability to operate at both 30,000 feet and ground level.
- Comfortable navigating ambiguity, driving change, and making bold decisions.
- Entrepreneurial spirit with a bias for action and a commitment to delivering measurable outcomes.
