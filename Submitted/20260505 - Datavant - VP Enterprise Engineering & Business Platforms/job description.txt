Datavant is hiring a Vice President, Enterprise Engineering & Business Platforms to unify architecture and mature operations across the company's enterprise business platform layer: finance, CRM, integration, automation, and customer identity.

Role focus:
- Own engineering and architecture for revenue-critical enterprise systems.
- Cover financial platforms, CRM, integration middleware, automation infrastructure, and customer-facing identity.
- Support systems processing hundreds of millions of dollars in revenue, complex healthcare workflows, and dozens of applications across a growing and acquisition-influenced ecosystem.
- Bring architectural clarity, durable ownership, integration discipline, and long-term cohesion to a fragmented enterprise platform layer.
- Make definitive architecture and vendor decisions, simplify and consolidate systems, establish governance, and build a high-performing organization for regulated healthcare enterprise platforms.

Key responsibilities:
- Define enterprise business platform architecture and governance.
- Establish system boundaries, integration patterns, API management, middleware strategy, and event-driven patterns.
- Prevent platform fragmentation and over-customization, especially around Salesforce and financial systems.
- Own engineering architecture for billing, revenue recognition, contract management, and financial reporting.
- Partner with Finance leadership on scalable, audit-ready, operationally resilient systems.
- Own CRM and Salesforce architecture governance, data standards, and change control.
- Govern vendors and implementation partners to reduce architectural drift and technical debt.
- Establish middleware as a first-class platform capability and reduce contractor dependency.
- Govern enterprise automation: managed file transfer, RPA, AI-enabled workflow initiatives, and contact-center automation.
- Consolidate and harden customer-facing authentication and authorization.
- Lead and develop directors and managers across financial systems, CRM, middleware, and automation.
- Build senior enterprise platform engineering capacity globally.

Requirements:
- 15+ years in technology leadership.
- 5+ years leading enterprise engineering or platform organizations at VP or senior director level.
- Complex multi-platform enterprise ecosystems.
- Integration architecture, middleware, APIs, and event-driven systems.
- COTS platforms such as Salesforce, NetSuite, or equivalent, with governance against over-customization.
- Financial or revenue systems modernization in high-growth or acquisition-driven environments.
- Consolidating fragmented ownership models and building durable engineering organizations.
- Regulated or compliance-heavy industries, healthcare, life sciences, or financial services preferred.
- Clear communication, stakeholder alignment, and definitive architecture/vendor decisions under ambiguity.
- Geographically distributed teams and significant vendor ecosystems.
